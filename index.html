Welcome to Step Sync.

Step Sync is a browser-based interactive audio sequencer. It allows you to program, play and manipulate audio loops in real-time.

Each row on the Loop Sequencing screen will let you maniplulate the sequencing, loop length, volume and FX send of a an audio loop.

Click the buttons in the column to the left to enter "Edit Mode," where you can edit that row's sequence and FX levels.
